How BYN works

Each round you get 1,000 nuts — virtual credits. Stake them on real sporting events before lockout.

01

Get your nuts

You receive 1,000 nuts at the start of every round. Winnings carry forward — build your balance across the season.

02

Pick your outcomes

Choose from real upcoming fixtures. Odds are seeded from bookmaker data, then move as other users bet — just like a real market.

03

Beat the minimum

You must stake at least 50% of your balance before lockout. Stake less and the shortfall is forfeited — it keeps everyone in the game.

04

Climb the table

Winnings roll into your next round. At the end of the season, leaderboards reset — everyone starts fresh.
